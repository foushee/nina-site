Nina Foushee is an enthusiastic, engaged, and skilled writing tutor and coach. Nina worked as a writing tutor for three years in the Stanford University Hume Writing Center from 2012-2015. In that time, Nina worked with undergraduate and graduate students on academic essays and research papers across the disciplines, and on personal statements and fellowship application materials. Nina’s strengths as a tutor lie in her ability to explain complex writing concepts intelligently and clearly to writers, her on-the-spot problem solving, and her talent with helping students build their skills and confidence in writing.

Dr. Julia Bleakney, Former Director of the Hume Center for Writing and Speaking, Stanford University
